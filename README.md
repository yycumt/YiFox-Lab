## 🇨🇳 关于本项目

本项目记录围绕文本、情绪、场景、风格与文化材料等展开的再创作实验。作品可能来自古典诗文、戏剧文本、历史叙事、哲学观念，也可能只是来自某种具体的情绪状态、人物姿态、声音质感或场景想象等。

这里不预设固定流派，也不预设单一思想体系。不同作品可以严肃，也可以戏谑；可以重思想，也可以重情绪；可以依托古典文本，也可以转向更自由的声音实验。

YiFox-Lab 更像是一种游走式的创作方法：在文学、历史、哲学、民间想象、多源文化材料、声音艺术、图像艺术与现代 AI 生成之间，寻找新的表达可能。

---

## 策展式创作

我将这些作品理解为一种个人的“策展式创作”。

创作过程通常包括材料选择、文本整理、风格设定、AI 生成、版本筛选、封面构想与最终归档。

在这个过程中，AI 是音频与图像生成工具、试错实验系统和不可预测的合作者。创作者负责方向设定、文本编排与再创作、必要的音频和图像编辑、审美判断与作品整理。

本项目并不追求一种固定方法。每首作品都可以有自己的重心：文本、情绪、场景、观念、声音，或只是某种难以精确定义但值得保留下来的气质。

## 作品命名

YiFox-Lab 中的作品命名通常采用以下结构：

```text
原作 · 本作演绎（可选标签）
```

其中，“原作”用于标明作品的核心来源、文本锚点或主要意象；“本作演绎”用于提示本次再创作的理解方向、情绪结构、场景设定或声音气质；“可选标签”则可以补充流派、风格、场景或版本信息。

这一命名方式并非严格模板，而是一种归档原则：用标题同时标明来源、演绎方向与作品气质。

对于并非直接改编自单一原典的作品，主标题也可以来自核心意象、人物状态、声音场景或概念母题。命名规则服务于作品的识别、归档与再表达，而不是限制作品本身。

## 业余，但严肃

Dr. 乙狐并非职业音乐制作人。

“业余”在这里并不意味着随意，而是一种为了兴趣、表达和探索而持续投入的创作状态。

YiFox-Lab 的作品可能并不统一：理性结构与感性经验、古典文本与现代生成技术、严肃思考与荒诞趣味，都会在这里并置、摩擦和融合。

这种不稳定性不是需要被消除的问题，而是本项目的生成机制之一。

## 🇬🇧 About the Project

This project documents creative experiments centered on texts, emotions, scenes, styles, and cultural materials. The works may come from classical poetry, dramatic texts, historical narratives, philosophical ideas, or simply from a specific emotional state, character posture, sonic texture, or imagined scene.

There is no fixed genre or single intellectual framework here. Different works may be serious or satirical; idea-driven or emotion-driven; rooted in classical texts or moving toward freer sonic experimentation.

YiFox-Lab is closer to a nomadic creative method: seeking new expressive possibilities among literature, history, philosophy, folk imagination, diverse cultural materials, sonic art, visual art, and modern AI generation.

---

## Curatorial Creation

I understand these works as a form of personal **curatorial creation**.

The process usually involves selecting materials, arranging texts, defining stylistic directions, generating with AI, evaluating versions, imagining cover concepts, and archiving the final result.

In this process, AI functions as a tool for audio and image generation, a trial-and-error experimental system, and an unpredictable collaborator. The creator remains responsible for direction, textual arrangement and re-creation, necessary audio and image editing, aesthetic judgment, and final organization.

This project does not follow a fixed formula. Each work may have its own center of gravity: text, emotion, scene, concept, sound, or simply an indefinable quality worth preserving.

---

## Naming System

Works in YiFox-Lab usually follow this naming structure:

```text
Source Work · Reinterpretation (Optional Tag)
```

The “Source Work” identifies the core reference, textual anchor, or central image of the piece. The “Reinterpretation” suggests the direction of the new work: its emotional structure, scene design, interpretive angle, or sonic character. The “Optional Tag” may indicate genre, style, scene, or version information.

This naming system is not a rigid template, but an archival principle: the title should indicate the source, the reinterpretive direction, and the artistic character of the work.

For works not directly adapted from a single source text, the main title may also come from a central image, character state, sonic scene, or conceptual motif. The naming system exists to support identification, archiving, and re-expression, rather than to restrict the work itself.

## Amateur, Seriously

Dr. 乙狐 is not a professional music producer.

“Amateur” here does not mean casual. It refers to a state of continuous creation driven by interest, expression, and exploration.

The works in YiFox-Lab may not appear uniform: rational structures and affective experience, classical texts and modern generative technologies, serious reflection and absurd humor may coexist, collide, and merge here.

This instability is not a problem to be eliminated, but one of the generative mechanisms of the project.

## 📂 Project Structure

```text
YiFox-Lab/
├── lyrics/          # 纯歌词文本 / Clean lyric sheets
├── works/           # 单曲完整档案 / Full track archives
├── docs/            # 方法论、说明文档等 / Methodology and documentation
├── assets/          # 封面图与静态资源 / Cover art and static resources
│   └── covers/
└── README.md
```

`lyrics/` 用于保存纯歌词文本，方便阅读、复制和平台提交。  
`works/` 用于保存更完整的单曲档案，包括元数据、翻译、创作说明、原典来源和技术记录等。

`lyrics/` contains clean lyric sheets for direct reading and platform use.  
`works/` contains fuller track archives, including metadata, translations, concepts, source notes, and technical records.
